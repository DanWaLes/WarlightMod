This Mod adds a couple of User-Created Cards. Already added:
  -Pestilence Card
    -> Adds Pestilence Cards to the game. A player you play a Pestilence Card on will lose [Pestilence Strength] troops on every Territory in the end of his next turn. Territories with 0 armies left will go Neutral.
