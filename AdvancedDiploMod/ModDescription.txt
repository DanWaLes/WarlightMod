There are a lot of problems with commerce games, so you shouldn't use this mod at the moment with commerce games.
Don't use this mod with any other mod with a notifications system, else it may happen that the notifications don't show up.

This Mod adds:
War/Peace System:
 Â -Attacks DON'T count as declaration
  -After you declared War on someone, you have to wait one turn before you can attack him

  -Players you are allied with automatically declare war on players declaring war on you(their allies won't declare war on you)

  -Alliances can be either public or private(depends on the settings)
Ingame currency:
  -You get money for(each item depends on the settings):       - killing armies
                                                               - taking territories
                                                               - every turn
                                                               - taking bonuses
                                                               - Game start
  -You can sell territories
  -You can buy armies of the shop for a definded price
  -You can gift money to other players
  -If the setting basic money system is activated, you get the money in the next turn

  -AIs won't declare war on players(depends on settings)
